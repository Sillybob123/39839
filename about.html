Welcome to Torah Study Circle

Torah Study Circle is an interactive platform designed to make weekly Torah study accessible, engaging, and meaningful for everyone. Whether you're beginning your journey or deepening your existing practice, our website brings ancient wisdom into the modern age.

Our Mission

We believe that Torah study should be:

  • Accessible - Available to anyone, anywhere, on any device
  • Interactive - Engaging with clickable verses and keyword definitions
  • Comprehensive - Combining traditional and modern commentaries
  • Beautiful - A reading experience that honors the sacred text

Features

Weekly Portions

Follow along with the traditional Torah reading cycle, with automatic highlighting of the current week's parsha.

Commentary

Access insights from classical commentators like Rashi and Ramban, alongside modern scholars like Rabbi Jonathan Sacks.

Hebrew Keywords

Click on highlighted Hebrew terms to understand their deeper meanings and linguistic significance.

Bilingual Text

Read the Torah in both Hebrew and English, with proper right-to-left Hebrew text rendering.

How to Use This Website

  1. Select a Parsha: Use the dropdown menu to choose which Torah portion you'd like to study.
  2. Read the Text: Each verse appears in both English and Hebrew for parallel reading.
  3. Click for Commentary: Verses with a blue border have commentary. Click to view insights from traditional and modern sources.
  4. Explore Keywords: Words with dotted blue underlines have definitions. Click to learn more about Hebrew terms.
  5. Navigate: Use the Previous/Next buttons to move through the Torah portions in order.

Data Sources

Torah Text: All Hebrew and English Torah text is provided by Sefaria.org , a free living library of Jewish texts. Sefaria is a non-profit organization dedicated to building the future of Jewish learning in an open and participatory way.

Commentary & Definitions: The commentary and keyword definitions are curated from traditional sources and modern scholars, providing deep insights into the weekly Torah portion.
